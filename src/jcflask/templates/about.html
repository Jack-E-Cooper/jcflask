About Me

Hello, I'm John Cooper—a former Signals Captain in the Canadian Army with a passion for all-hazards security.

My career bridges information security, physical security, policy development, training, ethics, and continuity management. I am dedicated to My professional path began in the Canadian Armed Forces, where I developed a unique blend of technical, operational, and leadership skills. From serving as an Electronic Warfare Troop Commander and Deputy Operations Officer to functioning as a trusted Information Systems Security Officer, I gained deep experience in risk management, threat analysis, secure communications, and crisis response. Each role challenged me to think critically, act decisively, and uphold the highest standards of integrity and ethics, even in high-pressure situations.

With academic foundations including a Master of Public Safety in National Security and a Bachelor of Computer Science, I have always pursued excellence—whether on the field or in the lab. I am currently furthering my expertise as a CISSP candidate, an endeavor that underlines my commitment to mastering the evolving landscape of cybersecurity and all-hazards security.

Professional Highlights

  • Strategic Leadership: Led diverse teams in the Canadian Armed Forces, coordinating security operations, guiding technical projects, and providing critical instruction on communications, incident response, and operational risk management.
  • All-Hazards Security Expertise: Developed and implemented risk assessments, managed security audits, enhanced physical security protocols, and led policy and training initiatives to address evolving threats across digital and physical domains.
  • Ethics & Training: Championed a culture of ethical conduct and continuous learning, delivering training on security best practices, policy compliance, and organizational resilience.
  • Recognition & Awards: Honored with accolades like the Task Force Latvia Commander’s Commendation and the NATO Special Service Medal, reflecting a commitment to excellence and an unwavering focus on detail, strategy, and ethical leadership.
