About Me

Hello, I'm John Cooper—a former Signals Captain in the Canadian Army with a passion for security, technology, and leadership. My career has been an evolving journey from the rigorous environments of military operations to the ever-dynamic realm of cybersecurity. Today, I strive to blend technical proficiency with strategic insight to safeguard information and empower teams in complex, global environments.

My Journey

My professional path began in the Canadian Armed Forces, where I honed a unique blend of technical, operational, and leadership skills. From serving as an Electronic Warfare Troop Commander and Deputy Operations Officer to functioning as a trusted Information Systems Security Officer, I developed a deep understanding of risk management, threat analysis, and secure communications. Each role challenged me to think critically, act decisively, and maintain the highest standards of integrity even in high-pressure situations.

With academic foundations including a Master of Public Safety in National Security and a Bachelor of Computer Science, I have always pursued excellence—whether on the field or in the lab. I am currently furthering my expertise as a CISSP candidate, an endeavor that underlines my commitment to mastering the evolving landscape of cybersecurity.

Professional Highlights

  • Strategic Leadership: Leading diverse teams in the Canadian Armed Forces, I coordinated security operations, guided technical projects, and provided critical instruction on communications and incident response.
  • Technical Expertise: From developing risk assessments and managing security audits to navigating the complexities of IT transitions during the COVID-19 pandemic, my career has been defined by my ability to blend technical know-how with practical, real-world solutions.
  • Recognition & Awards: Honored with accolades like the Task Force Latvia Commander’s Commendation and the NATO Special Service Medal, my work reflects a commitment to excellence and an unwavering focus on detail and strategy.

My Mission

In today’s rapidly evolving digital world, I believe that security isn’t just a technical challenge—it’s a strategic imperative that requires clear vision and relentless innovation. Through my work and this portfolio, I aim to share my experiences, insights, and projects with fellow professionals, enthusiasts, and organizations that value secure, resilient, and ethically-driven technology solutions.

I invite you to explore my projects, read my thoughts on emerging cybersecurity trends, and join me on this journey of continuous learning and innovation. Together, we can build safer, smarter, and more secure technological ecosystems.
